Roasted halloumi and peppers served with lentils

Description

Quick and classic.

Ingredients

  • 3 peppers
  • 120g halloumi
  • 1 pack of ready-made lentils or grains
  • 15ml olive oil

Steps

  1. Warm the olive oil in a pan
  2. Add the peppers, chopped into thin strips
  3. When they are almost cooked, add the halloumi, cut into chunks
  4. Serve with the packaged lentils or grains
